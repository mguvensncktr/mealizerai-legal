## 4. Personal information we collect

We collect only the information needed to provide the app, generate meal plans, and manage subscriptions and notifications.

### 4.1 Information you provide (profile and preferences)

You may provide the following profile information:

- Name and surname; gender; age  
- Height; weight; goal (lose/maintain/gain); optional target weight  
- Diet type; allergies; disliked foods  
- Daily activity level  
- Time available for cooking; meals per day; meal budget; cooking skill level  
- Water intake frequency and related lifestyle preferences  

**Health-related information.** Some profile fields (for example weight and allergy information) may be considered sensitive or health-related under certain laws. We collect and use this information only to generate meal plans and provide app functionality.

### 4.2 Third-party AI processing (Google Gemini) - detailed

Mealizer AI uses a third-party AI service (**Google Gemini**) to generate meal plans. This section describes how the AI feature works and what data is involved.

**When AI processing happens**  
AI processing occurs when you actively use AI features, including (a) generating a meal plan and (b) chatting with AI to revise or refine an existing plan or meal. For example, you may ask the AI to swap ingredients, adjust portions, regenerate a day, or tailor meals based on your preferences.

**What data is sent to Google Gemini**  
We send the information needed to fulfill your request, which may include:

- Diet type, goals, and daily activity level  
- Allergies and disliked foods (to avoid unsafe ingredients)  
- Height, weight, age (to tailor portions and nutritional direction)  
- Meals per day, cooking time constraints, budget, and cooking skill level  
- Water intake preference and other lifestyle preferences you provided  
- If you entered name or gender, these fields may also be included in the profile data sent for personalization (they are not required to generate a plan).  
- Your AI chat messages and instructions (what you type in the chat).  
- Relevant context from your existing meal plan/meal (for example selected meals, ingredients, and steps) that is necessary to fulfill your request.  

**What data is NOT sent to the AI provider**

- Your push notification token  
- Your in-app purchase payment details (Apple/Google process payments)  
- Photos, contacts, or precise location (we do not collect these)  

**What we receive back**  
We receive AI-generated content (e.g., meal plans, revised meal suggestions, ingredient substitutions, updated steps, and chat responses).

**How we store AI-related data**  
We store your profile information and the resulting meal plan (including revisions) in our database so you can view it later. We do **not** store your AI chat messages or chat history; after a chat-based revision, we store only the updated plan/meal content.

**AI provider retention and human review**  
Google states that for abuse monitoring, it retains prompts, contextual information, and generated outputs for **55 days**. If content is flagged by safety filters, authorized Google employees may review the flagged prompts or outputs. Google also states that data retained for abuse monitoring is used solely for policy enforcement and is not used to train or fine-tune models.

**We use paid AI services for end-user requests**  
We use paid AI services for processing end-user meal plan requests and do not intentionally send sensitive, confidential, or unnecessary information beyond what is required to generate your plan.

**AI outputs may be inaccurate**  
AI-generated meal plans can contain mistakes. Do not rely on Mealizer AI for medical, allergy, or safety-critical decisions. Always verify ingredients and consult a professional when needed.

**Your explicit permission for third-party AI**  
Before we share personal information with a third-party AI provider, we will clearly disclose this in the app and ask for your explicit permission. If you do not grant permission, you may not be able to use AI meal plan generation features.

### 4.3 Push notification data

If you enable notifications, we store a **push token** (for example an Expo Push Token) to deliver push notifications. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

### 4.4 Subscription and paywall data (in-app purchases)

We offer subscriptions through in-app purchases on the Apple App Store and Google Play. Payment is processed by Apple or Google. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

We may store limited purchase information needed to validate entitlements:

- Subscription status (active/inactive)  
- Purchase and receipt identifiers as needed to confirm access to paid features  

### 4.5 App diagnostics and internal logs

We maintain a simple internal logging system for reliability and security. These logs may include:

- A timestamp, event type (for example onboarding completed), and app version  
- Error messages or crash-like diagnostics (when something fails)  
- The random unique identifier for your app installation (to associate logs with your data)  

We do not use these logs for advertising or cross-app tracking. We do not run third-party analytics SDKs.
